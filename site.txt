mw-parser-output" lang="en" dir="ltr"><p>Fish are the core item and primary objective of <i>Fisch</i>. Each <a href="/wiki/Category:Locations" class="mw-redirect" title="Category:Locations">location</a> hosts it's unique set of fish, which must be collected to complete the bestiary. Many locations also contain "sublocations" where a different pool of fish may be caught (i.e. <a href="/wiki/Moosewood" title="Moosewood">Moosewood</a> pond). Each fish may have an associated weather condition, time of day, season, bait, and specific fishing location that will boost the odds of obtaining it, making bestiary completion easier. Fish may also be caught with a <a href="/wiki/Mutations" title="Mutations">mutation</a>, increasing it's value and changing it's appearance. Fish have an associated rarity, however the exact odds and mechanics behind determining catches is unknown.
</p><p>All Fish are catalogued and categorised in the <a href="/wiki/Items#Bestiary" title="Items">Bestiary</a>, completing full categories rewards <a href="/wiki/Fishing_Rods#Bobbers" title="Fishing Rods">Bobbers</a>
</p>
